S&R Shopping Mall Sales Analysis Dashboard

The project creates an interactive sales analysis dashboard for H&R Shopping Mall. The dashboard visualizes key metrics such as top-selling products, peak shopping hours, and customer behavior trends, helping management make data-driven decisions while keeping a consistent branded presentation.

QMRecord: Public Health Data Analysis at Quezon Medical Center

The project focuses on analyzing health data from Quezon Medical Center in Lucena City, including COVID-19 cases, hospital admissions, and vaccination records. Using data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and visualization techniques, trends and patterns in patient care and public health outcomes are uncovered.
